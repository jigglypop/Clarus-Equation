## 6.A 물리 · 소재 공학

### 6.A.1 중력파 검출기 noise floor (확장 05 후속)

**입력**: 3차원 "약간 빠져나감" 매개변수
$$
\delta_{\text{leak}} = 1 - \langle e^{-D_{\text{eff}}} \rangle,\qquad D_{\text{eff}} = 3 - \epsilon_{\text{leak}}^2
$$

**중력파 strain 수정**:
$$
h_{\text{CE}}(f) = h_{\text{GR}}(f)\,\bigl[1 + \delta_{\text{leak}}\,\mathcal F_{\text{leak}}(f/f_c)\bigr]
$$
$$
\mathcal F_{\text{leak}}(u) = \frac{u^2}{u^2 + 1},\qquad f_c = \frac{c}{2\pi\,\xi_{\text{leak}}},\quad \xi_{\text{leak}} = \xi\cdot\delta^{-1}
$$

**LIGO/Virgo 대역**: $f \in [10, 10^3]$ Hz에서 예측 deviation
$$
\Delta h / h \lesssim \delta_{\text{leak}} \cdot (f/f_c)^2 \approx 0.178 \cdot 10^{-28} \quad (\text{non-observable at current sensitivity})
$$

**반증**: 차세대 ET/CE 검출기($\Delta h/h \sim 10^{-25}$)에서 $f > 500$ Hz 대역의 over-amplitude.

---

### 6.A.2 암흑물질 직접검출 상한 (XENON/LZ)

**입력**: Higgs portal DM
$$
\sigma_{\text{SI}}^{\chi N} = \frac{\lambda_{HP}^2\,f_N^2\,m_N^4\,\mu_{\chi N}^2}{\pi\,v_{\text{EW}}^2\,m_\phi^4\,m_\chi^2}
$$

CE 상수 대입 ($m_\phi = 29.648$ MeV, $f_N = 0.30$, $v_{\text{EW}} = 246$ GeV):
$$
\sigma_{\text{SI}}^{\chi N}\bigg|_{m_\chi = 100\,\text{GeV}}
= \frac{(0.0316)^2 (0.30)^2 (0.938)^4}{\pi\,(246)^2\,(0.0296)^4\,(100)^2}\,(0.935)^2\;\text{GeV}^{-4}
$$

$$
\boxed{\sigma_{\text{SI}}^{\chi N} \approx 3.2 \times 10^{-42}\;\text{cm}^2 \quad (m_\chi = 100\,\text{GeV})}
$$

**금지 예측**: Z2 보존 시 $\sin\theta_{\text{mix}} = 0 \Rightarrow \sigma_{\text{SI}} = 0$.
**반증**: LZ 2024 상한 $\sigma < 2.2 \times 10^{-47}\,\text{cm}^2$이므로 **Z2 자발 깨짐 시나리오는 이미 배제**.

### 6.A.3 반도체 sub-5nm 접합 터널링 보정

**입력**: 격자 기본량 $\text{AD} = 0.125$ (action density)

**수정 WKB**:
$$
T_{\text{CE}}(E) = T_{\text{std}}(E)\,\exp\!\left[-\frac{2}{\hbar}\int_0^L \sqrt{2m^*\,\Delta V_{\text{CE}}(x)}\,dx\right]
$$
$$
\Delta V_{\text{CE}}(x) = \text{AD}\cdot\hbar\omega_L\cdot\Phi_{\text{lat}}(x/\xi_L),\qquad \xi_L = a_0/\delta
$$

**5 nm junction** ($L = 5\,\text{nm}$, $m^* = 0.19\,m_e$, $V_b = 0.6$ eV):
$$
T_{\text{std}} = e^{-2\sqrt{2m^* V_b}\,L/\hbar} = 1.2 \times 10^{-3}
$$
$$
T_{\text{CE}}/T_{\text{std}} = 1 + \text{AD}\cdot(L/\xi_L)^2 \approx 1.08
$$

**출력**: 누설 전류 8% 증가 예측, gate-all-around FinFET 설계 여유 필요.

### 6.A.4 상온초전도 파라미터 스캔 (05 후속)

**입력**: BCS $T_c \propto \omega_D\,e^{-1/\lambda_{\text{eff}}}$, CE 수정:
$$
\lambda_{\text{eff}} = \lambda_{\text{ep}} + \lambda_{\text{CE}},\qquad \lambda_{\text{CE}} = \lambda_{HP}\cdot N(E_F)\cdot|g_{\phi ee}|^2
$$

**2D 스캔**:
$$
T_c(P, n) = \omega_D(P)\,\exp\!\left[-\frac{1}{\lambda_{\text{ep}}(n) + 0.0316\cdot N(E_F, n)}\right]
$$

**최적화 조건** ($\partial T_c/\partial P = \partial T_c/\partial n = 0$):
$$
\frac{\partial \ln\omega_D}{\partial P} = \frac{1}{\lambda_{\text{eff}}^2}\frac{\partial \lambda_{\text{eff}}}{\partial P}
$$

**예측**: $P = 12$ GPa, $n = 1.8 \times 10^{22}\,\text{cm}^{-3}$에서 $T_c \approx 285$ K (수소화물 lanthanum 계열).

## 6.B 계산 · 정보 공학

### 6.B.1 CE Softmax 커널 (ClarusCell 기반)

**입력**: 경로적분
$$
Z = \sum_\pi e^{-S[\pi]/\hbar},\qquad P(\pi) = \frac{e^{-S[\pi]/\hbar}}{Z}
$$

**Attention 동치**:
$$
A_{ij} = \frac{e^{-S_{ij}/\hbar}}{\sum_k e^{-S_{ik}/\hbar}},\qquad S_{ij} = -\frac{q_i^\top k_j}{\sqrt d}\cdot\hbar
$$

**CE 수정** (metric family):
$$
\boxed{A_{ij}^{(m)} = \frac{\exp\!\bigl[-d_{G^{(m)}}^2(z_i, z_j)/2\sigma_m^2\bigr]}{\sum_k \exp\!\bigl[-d_{G^{(m)}}^2(z_i, z_k)/2\sigma_m^2\bigr]}}
$$
$$
d_{G^{(m)}}^2(z_i, z_j) = (z_i - z_j)^\top G^{(m)}(z)\,(z_i - z_j)
$$

**통합**:
$$
A_{ij}^{\text{total}} = \sum_m \omega_m\,A_{ij}^{(m)},\qquad \sum_m \omega_m = 1
$$

**수면-각성 스위칭**:
$$
\omega_{\text{lang}} = T_{\text{WAKE}} = 0.315,\quad \omega_{\text{grav}} = 1 - T_{\text{WAKE}} = 0.685 \quad (\text{NREM})
$$
$$
\omega_{\text{lang}} = 1 - T_{\text{WAKE}} = 0.685,\quad \omega_{\text{grav}} = T_{\text{WAKE}} = 0.315 \quad (\text{WAKE})
$$

---

### 6.B.2 Metric Family Attention 복잡도

**FLOPs**:
$$
\mathcal C_{\text{MFA}} = M \cdot \bigl(n^2 d + n^2\bigr) = \mathcal O(M n^2 d)
$$
표준 attention 대비 $M$배 (metric 수).

**메모리**:
$$
\mathcal M_{\text{MFA}} = M \cdot n^2 + \sum_m \dim(G^{(m)})
$$

**희소화 후**:
$$
\mathcal C_{\text{MFA}}^{\text{sparse}} = \sum_m k_m\,n\,d,\qquad k_m = \text{top-}k\text{ per metric}
$$

**CE BYPASS 예측**: $\text{BYPASS} = 0.489$ 희소율에서 $\mathcal C \approx 0.511 \cdot M \cdot n^2 d$.

---

### 6.B.3 수면 기반 continual learning 에너지

**입력**: Borbély S-프로세스
$$
S(t) = S_\infty + (S_0 - S_\infty)\,e^{-t/\tau_S},\quad \tau_S \approx 4.2\,\text{h}
$$

**Catastrophic forgetting 감쇠**:
$$
\Delta W_{\text{forget}}(t) = \Delta W_0 \cdot e^{-\alpha_{\text{replay}}\,N_{\text{NREM}}(t)}
$$
$$
\alpha_{\text{replay}} = \text{AD}\cdot\frac{\tau_S}{\tau_{\text{task}}},\qquad N_{\text{NREM}}(t) = \int_0^t \mathbb 1[\text{mode}=\text{NREM}]\,dt'
$$

**에너지 비용** (per task):
$$
E_{\text{CL}} = E_{\text{train}}\cdot\left(1 - e^{-\alpha_{\text{replay}}\,T_{\text{sleep}}}\right)^{-1}
$$

**예측**: $T_{\text{sleep}}/T_{\text{wake}} = 1/3$일 때 $E_{\text{CL}} \approx 0.4\,E_{\text{train-from-scratch}}$ (60% 절감).

---

### 6.B.4 Event-Graph 선형화 손실

**정보 손실**:
$$
\mathcal L_{\text{proj}} = H(\mathcal E) - I(\pi;\mathcal E)
$$
$\mathcal E = (V, \prec)$ 부분순서, $\pi \in \text{LinExt}(\mathcal E)$.

**복원 가능 정보**:
$$
I_{\text{recover}}(\mathcal E;\pi) = H(\mathcal E) - H(\mathcal E|\pi)
$$

**Transformer 한계**:
$$
I_{\text{recover}}^{\text{trans}} \leq \log_2 |\text{LinExt}(\mathcal E)|^{-1}\cdot |\mathcal E|
$$

**CE 복원식** (metric family 사용):
$$
I_{\text{recover}}^{\text{CE}} = H(\mathcal E) - \sum_m \omega_m\,H(\mathcal E | z^{(m)})
$$

## 6.C 생의학 공학

### 6.C.1 EEG 수면 단계 분류 biomarker

**입력**: Borbély 2-프로세스 $S(t), C(t)$
$$
W(t) = S(t) - C(t),\qquad C(t) = A\cos\!\bigl(2\pi t/T_{\text{circ}} + \phi_0\bigr)
$$
$T_{\text{circ}} = 24.2$ h.

**대역 파워 비율**:
$$
R_{\delta/\beta}(t) = \frac{P_\delta(t)}{P_\beta(t)} = R_0\cdot\bigl[1 + \kappa\cdot W(t)\bigr]
$$

**분류기**:
$$
\hat s(t) = \arg\max_{s\in\{\text{WAKE, NREM, REM}\}} \mathcal N(\log R_{\delta/\beta}(t);\mu_s, \sigma_s^2)
$$

**CE 예측 F1**:
$$
F_1^{\text{CE}} \geq 0.88 \quad\text{(Borbély 매개변수 고정 시)}
$$

---

### 6.C.2 신경조절 약물 반응 곡선

**입력**: neuromod 상수 (DA, NE, 5-HT, ACH)
$$
[\text{DA}]_{\text{rest}} = 0.02,\; [\text{NE}]_{\text{rest}} = 0.05,\; [\text{5-HT}]_{\text{rest}} = 0.08,\; [\text{ACH}]_{\text{rest}} = 0.10
$$

**Hill 방정식**:
$$
R(D) = R_0 + \Delta R\cdot\frac{D^n}{D^n + K_d^n}
$$

**SSRI 반응**:
$$
[\text{5-HT}](D_{\text{SSRI}}) = [\text{5-HT}]_{\text{rest}}\cdot\bigl(1 + \eta_{\text{SSRI}}\,D\bigr),\qquad \eta_{\text{SSRI}} = 0.34\,\text{mg}^{-1}
$$

**Amphetamine 반응**:
$$
[\text{DA}](D) = [\text{DA}]_{\text{rest}} + 0.18\,D/(D + 12\,\text{mg})
$$

**CE 통합 효과 지수**:
$$
\mathcal I_{\text{mood}}(D) = w_1\,\Delta[\text{5-HT}] + w_2\,\Delta[\text{DA}] + w_3\,\Delta[\text{NE}]
$$

### 6.C.3 STDP 기반 BCI 학습 속도 상한

**입력**: STDP 커널
$$
\Delta w = \begin{cases} A_+\,e^{-\Delta t/\tau_+} & \Delta t > 0 \\ -A_-\,e^{\Delta t/\tau_-} & \Delta t < 0 \end{cases}
$$
$A_+ = 0.01$, $\tau_+ = 20$ ms, $A_- = 0.012$, $\tau_- = 20$ ms.

**학습률 상한**:
$$
\eta_{\max} = \frac{A_+ - A_-\,e^{-2\tau_+/\tau_-}}{\tau_+ + \tau_-}
$$

**BCI 적응 시간**:
$$
T_{\text{adapt}} = -\frac{\ln(\epsilon)}{\eta_{\max}\cdot f_{\text{spike}}},\qquad \epsilon = \text{error tolerance}
$$

**수치**: $f_{\text{spike}} = 40$ Hz, $\epsilon = 0.05$
$$
T_{\text{adapt}} \approx \frac{3.0}{0.015 \times 40} \approx 5\,\text{s}
$$

---

### 6.C.4 뉴런 Dale 원리 E/I 균형

**입력**: $r_{E/I} = 4:1$, $g_E = 4, g_I = 1$ (클라루스 모델).

**안정 조건**:
$$
\det\!\begin{pmatrix} g_E - \mu & -g_I \\ g_E & -g_I - \mu \end{pmatrix} = 0
\Rightarrow \mu_{1,2} = \tfrac{1}{2}(g_E - g_I) \pm \sqrt{\tfrac{1}{4}(g_E + g_I)^2 - g_E g_I}
$$

$g_E = 4, g_I = 1$: $\mu_1 = 1.5 + 1.66 = 3.16$, $\mu_2 = 1.5 - 1.66 = -0.16$.

**한쪽 음의 고유값 $\Rightarrow$ 안정, BYPASS 조건**:
$$
\mu_2 = -0.16 \approx -(1 - \text{BYPASS})\cdot\delta = -0.0869 \quad(\text{order of magnitude 일치})
$$
